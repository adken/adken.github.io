Programming short course and workshop on Deep learning and Computer Vision

The Programming short course and workshop on Deep Learning and Compute Vision was organised by Aristotle University of Thessaloniki from 24th to 26th August 2022. The three-day summer school offered participants both lectures and hands-on workshop sessions on CUDA Programming and Image classification using CNNs, OpenCV programming for object tracking, SSD object detection, drone cinematography with airsim, and video summarization with pytorch.
